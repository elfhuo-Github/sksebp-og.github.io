FIXES

For whatever reason, the game refuses to launch on some systems, unless the screen-resolution is instructed as a load parameter.
To fix this, all you have to do is add the following to the game:

Steam Version: Open Steam, then open the DooM 3 BFG game properties and click "Set Launch Options".
Insert the following line: +set r_fullscreen -1 +set r_windowHeight "2160" +set r_windowWidth "3840" +set r_windowY "0" +set r_windowX "0"
*Change "2160" and "3840" to match your screen-resolution.

GOG / non-Steam version: Right click the DooM 3 BFG desktop shortcut and click "Properties".
Insert the following line into target after the file-path:
+set r_fullscreen -1 +set r_windowHeight "2160" +set r_windowWidth "3840" +set r_windowY "0" +set r_windowX "0"
*Change "2160" and "3840" to match your screen-resolution.

GAME INFO

DooM 3 BFG Edition (2012) is a re-release of DooM 3 (2004), which includes the "Resurrection of Evil" expansion, plus DooM 1 & DooM 2, the two classics games that made first-person shooters popular. DooM 3 was a milestone for shooters back in 2004, which put the latest hardware at the time to the test. Even though the visuals are still decent today, time has caught up with parts of the game. This project aims to rejuvenate the graphics & effects with a few modifications, and some new HD textures. The project also has a new custom render which greatly improves lighting and image quality.
"Doom 3 BFG Edition" is a remastered version of the classic first-person shooter game "Doom 3", developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks. It was released in October 2012 for Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, and Microsoft Windows. This edition includes the original "Doom 3" game, as well as the "Resurrection of Evil" expansion pack and several new features.

One of the key features of "Doom 3 BFG Edition" is its improved graphics and lighting. The game features updated textures and models, as well as enhanced lighting and shadow effects. This gives the game a more polished and modern look, while still maintaining the atmosphere of the original game.

In terms of gameplay, "Doom 3 BFG Edition" stays true to the original game. Players take on the role of a marine who must fight his way through hordes of demons in order to uncover the secrets of the Martian research facility where the game takes place. The game features fast-paced and intense combat, as well as a variety of weapons and power-ups to help players in their fight.

One new feature added to "Doom 3 BFG Edition" is the "Armor-Lock" feature. This allows players to temporarily invincibility during combat, allowing them to get out of tight spots or make a strategic retreat. The edition also includes the "Lost Mission", which features eight new levels that were not included in the original release.

"Doom 3 BFG Edition" also includes the original "Doom" and "Doom II" games, allowing players to play through the classic games that started it all.

Overall, "Doom 3 BFG Edition" is a great remastered edition of a classic game. The improved graphics and lighting give the game a more modern look, while still maintaining the atmosphere of the original game. The new features, such as the "Armor-Lock" and "Lost Mission" add a new level of replayability to the game. Fans of the original game and newcomers alike will enjoy the intense and fast-paced gameplay that "Doom 3 BFG Edition" has to offer.
DooM 3 BFG Edition was launched in 2012, shortly after ID Soft came under the Bethesda umbrella. The original DooM 3 was released in 2004 by Activision, followed by the "Resurrection of Evil" expansion in 2005. The game was absolute state of the art at it's first release back in 2004. It was a highly anticipated follower to the biggest shooter classics of all times, DooM 1 & DooM 2. The games that revolutionized the first-person shooter genre. DooM 3 didn't have any new revolutionary ideas or technology, but it did managed to bring the DooM name to a leading position in the genre once again. The game has was, and still is, a very solid and well-made shooter with a simple yet immersive story.
DooM 3 BFG Edition includes the previously unreleased "Lost Missions". The lost missions are about the last surviving member of the Bravo team, which was ambushed by demons in Doom 3. The player is contacted by a scientist working on teleportation experiments, and asked to help him destroy an experimental teleport-array that was captured by demons. The player must first acquire some components to activate the teleportation system, then travel to the demons dimension and destroy the physical array.
