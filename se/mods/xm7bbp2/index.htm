ABOUT THIS MOD:

M7BBP2
MayheM-7 Bootyful-Body with Physics 2
New female body-model with clothing and armor replacement. Made to look best at weight 50, with a big difference from weight 0 to 100, making the people of Skyrim more diverse in sizes.

Mod author: Tore Andersen.

The M7BBP2 mod replaces the female body for all races, except Argonian, Khajiit and custom races. The BodySlide files are not necessary for the mod to work. They are optional files for use with Calient's BodySlide Tool, so you can make any armor fit the M7BBP2 body.
Note: There are 2 versions of the main file. Regular and Shaved. Choose one.
